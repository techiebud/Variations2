About Us

The Variations Codominium Association is located near the corner of Clairmont Road and Buford Highway in the historic town of Chamblee, Georgia. The community is about 12 miles from downtown Atlanta and is 1 mile northeast of the major interstate I-85. The neighborhood is conventiently located within walking distance of various restaurants and grocery stores.
