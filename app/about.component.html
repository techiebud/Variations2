About Us

The Variations Condominium Association is a community of 131 townhome-style condos in Chamblee, Georgia. The community is located near the corner of Clairmont Road and Buford Highway, about 12 miles northeast of downtown Atlanta and 1 mile from the major interstate I-85. The neighborhood is conveniently located within walking distance of various restaurants and grocery stores.


The Chamblee and Brookhaven/Oglethorpe Marta rail stations are both within 3 miles of the community. The #19 bus route has a stop located near the entrance to the complex on Clairmont Road.
