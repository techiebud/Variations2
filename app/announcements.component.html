Special Announcement

Voting Results from the December 12th, 2016 Annual HOA Meeting:

  • The 2017 Budget was accepted.
  • Three new board members were elected for two year terms - Bobby Ballinger, Susie Hawkins and Brett Kube.
  • The HOA Amendment did not pass because of less than 2/3 of homeowners voting. 60 votes were cast: 48 yes and 12 no. The Amendment will be considered again by the 2017 HOA Board.
Many, many thanks and appreciation to George Bahakel and Donna Gibbas for valiant work, long service and many hours of volunteerism for our community!
