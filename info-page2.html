The Variations Condominium Association, Inc

Bylaw Change Information: Electronic Communications 1

While the Georgia Condominium Act allows for electronic communications, it is not mentioned in the Variations Bylaws because they were written before electronic communications.

Explanation

This bylaw changes allows for notification of official meetings to be done through electronic communications. The HOA will always accommodate the few who do not use computers.

Why is this important to The Variations?

  • This saves time and approximately $200 of a large mailing for annual meeting.
  • It will give all homeowners the opportunity to participate in our elections; especially our neighbors who work overseas with CDC.
  • Greater accountability for the voting process. Each voter has an unique identifier which precludes multiple votes.

Endorsements

Rob Stein, our attorney states that most HOAs are using electronic communications as standard practice.
